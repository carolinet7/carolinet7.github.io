    Bears forever home journey

    On one cold December afternoon in Vermont, Bear was chosen by a family of 6 to come home to Massachusetts.Bear had a brown lab dad and yellow lab mom and many brothers and sisters.

    To the owners suprise, Bear who seemed calm and cuddly at first sight, was actually the craziest pup in the liter!

    She traveled all the way down to Massachusetts, where she met the rest of her family. She was greeted with many toys and a large backyard where she could chase squirrels and dig holes to nowhere.

    Bear had found her forever home and forever family!
