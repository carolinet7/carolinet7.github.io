    A day in the life of Bear

    Bear lives a pretty simple life, she does pretty much the same thing everyday. Luckily for her she never gets tired of it. Below is an order of her everyday routine from morning to night..

    1. Wakes up earlier than her owners would like
    2. Eats a yummy breakfast of wet dog food
    3. Goes outside for a bathroom break and to sniff around
    4. Walk!
    5. Rips apart every toy (and couch) in sight
    6. Nap!
    7. Outside playtime
    8. Walk!
    9. Dinner time
    10. Finally bed time!
